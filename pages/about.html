About

Welcome to Booktown Foundation

Booktown Foundation is a non-profit organization that provides books to the needy in multiple Indian languages. We believe everyone has the right to read, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality.

Money is not a big concern for us - we don't accept cash donations. If you want to help, just donate your old books that you don't need anymore. Our dream is to see a world where no one has to worry about accessing these valuable knowledge assets.

Our Mission

To promote literacy and love for reading by making books accessible to all, regardless of their economic background.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has equal opportunity to learn and grow through access to quality reading materials.
